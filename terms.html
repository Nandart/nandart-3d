Terms & Responsibilities

Welcome to NANdART. These terms establish the responsibilities, rights, and revenue sharing rules between the artists and the gallery. By submitting a work of art, the artist agrees to the following conditions:

1. Ownership and Copyright

All artworks remain the sole intellectual property of the artist. By submitting a work to NANdART, the artist grants the gallery a non-exclusive right to exhibit, mint, and sell the artwork as an NFT on their behalf.

2. Primary Sales

For the initial sale of any artwork:

  • 90% of the sale value is sent directly to the artist’s wallet.
  • 10% is retained by NANdART as a platform commission.

3. Secondary Sales (Resales)

In the event of future resales of the NFT through compatible platforms, the following royalties apply:

  • 6% of the resale value goes to the original artist.
  • 4% is sent to NANdART to support the gallery's curation and infrastructure.

4. Responsibilities of the Artist

By submitting an artwork, the artist agrees to:

  • Guarantee full authorship and originality of the submitted work.
  • Ensure that the content does not infringe upon any third-party rights.
  • Accept the gallery’s decision on whether the work is accepted for curation.
  • Specify whether the Premium Service is chosen for enhanced visibility in the central circle.

5. Responsibilities of NANdART

NANdART commits to:

  • Curate submitted artworks fairly and respectfully.
  • Display accepted works either in the main gallery (for Premium entries) or on the Web3 & NFT page.
  • Maintain a secure and transparent infrastructure for minting and transactions.
  • Respect the artist’s intellectual property at all times.

6. NFT Rights

When an artwork is minted as an NFT:

  • The buyer acquires a unique digital token representing the artwork’s authenticity.
  • No copyright or reproduction rights are transferred with the NFT.
  • All artistic rights remain fully with the original artist unless stated otherwise in writing.

7. Disclaimer & Termination

NANdART reserves the right to remove any artwork or NFT from the platform in the event of proven infringement, fraud, or misconduct. The artist may request removal of their work at any time, provided no NFT has yet been sold or minted.

By continuing to use the platform and submit works, the artist acknowledges these terms and accepts the shared values of transparency, respect, and artistic integrity.
