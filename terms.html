Artist Terms & Royalties

Every masterpiece submitted to NANdART enters a sacred pact — one that honours the creator’s voice and ensures timeless reward. This page outlines the principles we follow to protect and celebrate the rights of each artist within our curated gallery.

1. First Sale

When your artwork is accepted by the NANdART curators and minted as a certified NFT, it enters the premium marketplace. Upon its first sale:

  • You receive 90% of the full amount, directly to your connected wallet.
  • NANdART receives 10% to sustain the gallery’s infrastructure, curatorship and public presence.

2. Secondary Market Sales

When your NFT is resold in the future, the smart contract ensures that:

  • You receive 6% from every resale — eternally.
  • NANdART receives 4% to continue supporting the platform and maintaining visibility for all artists.

3. Your Responsibilities as an Artist

By submitting a piece to NANdART, you affirm that:

  • You are the original creator and hold all rights to the work.
  • Your work does not include AI-generated or plagiarised content.
  • Your submission reflects artistic integrity and does not violate ethical or legal norms.

4. NANdART Commitments to You

  • We honour your vision by curating with respect and aesthetic fidelity.
  • We ensure transparency and immutability through blockchain minting.
  • We never claim ownership of your work — only the right to exhibit it within our curated structure.
  • We protect your royalties through every transaction, present and future.

5. Premium Visibility (Optional)

If you choose the Premium Placement service, your approved work will be displayed prominently in the central visual circle of the gallery — visible to all visitors, collectors, and curators.

Standard approved submissions appear only on the Web3 & NFT page.

At NANdART, we believe in eternal echoes. Your art, your rights — always preserved.
